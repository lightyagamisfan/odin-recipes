Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Description

Lemon Ricotta Pancakes are a delightful twist on the classic pancake, offering a light and fluffy texture with a refreshing, zesty flavor. These pancakes are made with a blend of ricotta cheese, eggs, flour, and a touch of lemon zest, creating a batter that is rich yet airy. The ricotta cheese contributes to a soft, moist interior, while the lemon zest imparts a bright, citrusy note that balances the richness of the cheese.

When cooked, the pancakes form a golden-brown exterior, and when you cut into them, they reveal a tender, melt-in-your-mouth interior. The combination of creamy ricotta and tangy lemon creates a delicate flavor profile that is both indulgent and refreshing.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup ricotta cheese
  2. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 1/4 cup sugar (optional, depending on desired sweetness)
  5. 1/2 cup milk (whole milk or any milk substitute like almond or oat milk)
  6. 1 tbsp lemon zest (from about 1 lemon)
  7. 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  8. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. 1 tsp baking powder
  10. 1/2 tsp baking soda
  11. Pinch of salt
  12. Butter or oil (for greasing the pan)
  13. Powdered sugar (for dusting, optional)
  14. Fresh berries (blueberries, strawberries, etc. for serving, optional)
  15. Maple syrup or honey (for serving, optional)
  16. Whipped cream (optional, for serving)
